## ComplyGuard-AI Submission

### Executive Summary

**Problem:** Enterprise AI systems face regulatory liability (GDPR, HIPAA, EEOC, SOX) with no pre-deployment testing standard.

**Solution:** ComplyGuard-AI uses Gemini 3 Pro multimodal reasoning to test AI agent outputs across multiple compliance frameworks simultaneously, preventing lawsuits before deployment.

**Proof:** Live working MVP in Google AI Studio, tested across 4 industries (healthcare, finance, HR, insurance).

### Submission Details

**Repository:** [GitHub - ArjunFrancis/ComplyGuard-AI](https://github.com/ArjunFrancis/ComplyGuard-AI)
